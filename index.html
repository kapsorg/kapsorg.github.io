Korean American Professional Society (KAPS)

.

KAPS is a social and professional networking group for Koreans and Korean Americans in the San Francisco Bay Area. It was founded in 1992.

To receive email updates, introduce yourself to hello@kaps.org

Here are the best ways to get connected:

  1. Fill out the new member signup form.
    https://tinyurl.com/kaps-signup
  2. Add the “KAPS Organizers” account as a friend on Facebook. Then DM us right away.
    https://www.facebook.com/kaps.organizers
  3. Join the “KAPS Newbies” Facebook group.
    https://www.facebook.com/groups/kaps.newbies
  4. Follow @kaps_org on Instagram. Then DM us right away.
    https://www.instagram.com/kaps_org
  5. Connect with the “KAPS Organizers” account on LinkedIn.
